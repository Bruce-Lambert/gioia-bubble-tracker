Why include a total-market index?

Gioia's posts single out AI-adjacent stocks, but the overwhelming majority of Americans invest through broad, diversified index funds — not individual tech names. Showing VTI (Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF) alongside NVDA and META puts the "bubble" claim in the context that matters to most investors.
